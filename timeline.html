Timeline for Renting Apartments

This is a rough timeline for you to follow, but note that it differs from property to property

You must finalize roommates before you start Apartment Hunting, at around August/September

Why?
  1. Simpler lease process
  2. Shared Preferences
  3. Budget Clarity
  4. Easier Planning
How do I look for roommates?
  1. Use the UTK Off-campus Roommate Matching Site
  2. Use Roomsurf
  3. Join Facebook Groups
  4. Reach out to your friends and ask them

Immediately after you've finalized your roommates, start searching for Apartments at around August/September/October if you want it to be:

  1. Available for lease in the first place
  2. Within walking distance to campus
  3. Affordable

If you and your roommates got a property you desire, then:

What factors should I consider when finalizing an apartment?
  1. What are the costs associated with the leasing process?
  2. How do I ensure I am in a unit with my desired roommates?
  3. How many roommates are allowed in a unit/bedroom?
  4. Is renters' insurance required?
  5. How do I ensure I have been approved to live at a property?
  6. What is included in the rent? Electric, water, pest control, wi-fi, etc?
  7. Does the property allow sub-letting?
  8. What does the parking situation look like?
  9. When is the earliest/latest I can move in?
